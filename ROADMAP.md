# INSOMNIAC Roadmap

Roadmap order may change after manual playtesting. Each milestone should remain
small enough to verify with focused smoke tests and a playable manual test.

## v0.06-repeatable-orders

- Make the customer-order loop renewable after current orders are completed.
- Preserve order rewards, reputation, heat, and save/load behavior.
- Avoid introducing new major systems while stabilizing the repeatable loop.

## v0.07-project-docs-and-roadmap

- Record the current project state and milestone history.
- Maintain a practical roadmap, ideas backlog, and rules for coding agents.
- Keep documentation separate from gameplay implementation.

## v0.08-apartment-art-blockout

- Improve the apartment's visual layout and readability with an art blockout.
- Preserve existing interaction paths and gameplay functionality.
- Keep assets and rendering compatible with the web target.

## v0.09-more-orders-and-order-types

- Add more order content and introduce carefully scoped order variations.
- Balance payout, reputation, and heat rewards.
- Keep order generation and persistence reliable.

## v0.10-DPI-raid-prototype

- Prototype a small, readable DPI raid event.
- Connect raid risk to existing heat and DPI suspicion systems.
- Keep the prototype focused before considering combat or broader enforcement systems.

## v0.11-save-load-ui

- Add a clear in-game interface for saving and loading.
- Surface success, failure, and missing-save states to the player.
- Preserve compatibility with existing saves where practical.

## v0.12-intro-cutscene-prototype

- Prototype the opening sequence and title transition.
- Keep the sequence skippable and web friendly.
- Establish tone without blocking access to the playable loop.
